OUR STORY

A Bensenville Institution
Since 1976

For nearly five decades, Gina's has been serving up authentic Chicago-style eats from the same beloved spot on Irving Park Road in Bensenville — right near O'Hare International Airport. What started as a neighborhood hot dog stand has grown into a local legend.

We're proud members of the Vienna Beef Hall of Fame, recognized alongside Chicago's most celebrated hot dog institutions. Every dog is a genuine Vienna beef frank, loaded with fresh toppings on a warm steamed bun.

Our fries? Hand-cut fresh on site every single day. Our Italian ice? Homemade in-house with real fruit flavors. This is honest, no-frills food done right — the way it's been done here for generations.
